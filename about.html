About Us

Welcome to The Woods Barog!

Our Story

Crafting Beautiful Apartments Since '99

At The Woods Barog, we’re passionate about turning your Apartments into a true reflection of your personal style and a haven for relaxation and enjoyment.The Woods Barog offers you a chance to wake up to breathtaking hill views and snow-capped landscapes every day. Located just 60 km from Chandigarh and a short drive from Delhi-NCR, Barog is a tranquil hill station known for its untouched natural charm and calm ambiance, making it an ideal retreat from bustling city life. Our luxurious apartments, situated in the plush locality of Solan District, redefine second-home living. Perfectly blending comfort and nature, these residences provide an unparalleled opportunity to invest in Himachal's stunning terrain while enjoying easy access to the state’s picturesque destinations. At The Woods Barog, we understand that a second home is more than a vacation spot—it’s a haven for relaxation, rejuvenation, and connecting with nature. Discover a lifestyle that soothes your senses and celebrates the beauty of Himachal, right here in your own home.

Why Choose Us

Our Commitment to Excellence

01

STRUCTURE & CUSTOMISATION OF DESIGN

  • Earthquake resistant framed structure with infill brick walls.
  • 1.5 mtr. wide stairs steps, 1.5 mtr. wide corridor.
  • Living room and bedroom with large covered balconies for sitouts.
02

STATUS

  • The entire project is approved by Himuda and freehold.
  • Non-Himachalis can own and invest in the project without any hassles.
  • Affordable friendly homes with global comforts.
03

CONVENIENCE

  • High-speed automatic lift in every block with automatic rescue device.
  • Kone, OTIS or equivalent make.
  • Convenience shops for daily needs in the vicinity.
04

LOCATION/CONNECTIVITY

  • Located in the Shivaliks on National Highway-5 enroute to Shimla.
  • Only an hour’s drive from Panchkula.
05

SCENIC VIEWS

  • Panoramic views of Kasauli & Dakshai Hills and Naahan Valley.
  • 6000 thousand feet height (Shimla 7000’).
  • Occasional snowfall at the site.
