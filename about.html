Where is The Woods Barog located?

The Woods Barog is situated in Barog, a serene and picturesque hill station in the Solan district of Himachal Pradesh. It is well-connected, just 60 km from Chandigarh and easily accessible from Delhi-NCR via NH-22.

Can non-Himachali buyers purchase apartments in The Woods Barog?

Yes, The Woods Barog offers freehold apartments, meaning even non-Himachali buyers can legally own property here without any restrictions.

What types of apartments are available?

The project offers premium 1 & 2 BHK apartments with modern amenities. The buildings are either Ground + 3 floors or 6 floors, ensuring a comfortable and luxurious living experience.

What amenities are provided in The Woods Barog?

The property includes open parking, modern elevators, a gym, jacuzzi, table tennis, landscaped greens, and a daily-needs shopping area nearby. Additionally, it features rainwater harvesting and 78% open space, promoting a green and sustainable environment.

Why should I choose Barog over Shimla for my second home?

Barog offers a quieter, more untouched natural environment compared to Shimla, while still being close to major cities. It provides a pollution-free atmosphere, beautiful landscapes, and better investment potential with growing tourism and infrastructure.

Is The Woods Barog a good investment?

Yes! With rapid infrastructure development and increasing demand for second homes, investing in The Woods Barog ensures great returns, a peaceful retreat, and easy accessibility to Himachal‚Äôs scenic beauty.
