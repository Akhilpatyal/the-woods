About Us

Welcome to The Woods Barog!

Our Story

Crafting Beautiful Apartments Since '99

R. V. Nirmata Private Limited, led by Sh. R. K. Bhalla and Sh. Jiten Bhalla, is a renowned organization known for its exceptional team of architects, planners, and engineers. With over 50 years of experience, the company has executed a variety of projects across India. These include regional planning for townships like Greenfields, Faridabad, and the zoning plans for DLF Phase I, II, and III in Gurgaon. They have designed and supervised over 80 school buildings and more than 50 industrial projects, as well as 200 homes in Delhi NCR. The company’s expertise extends to large-scale ventures in housing, commercial complexes, and structural consultancy. At The Woods Barog, we offer a unique second-home experience, nestled in the peaceful hills of Solan District.Barog is the perfect retreat with stunning hill views and snow-capped landscapes. Our luxurious apartments combine comfort and nature, providing a serene escape from city life. We understand that a second home should be a haven for relaxation, and The Woods Barog offers just that. Experience the perfect blend of nature and modern living in Himachal Pradesh, all within easy reach of the state's picturesque destinations.

Our Commitment to Excellence

01

STRUCTURE & CUSTOMISATION OF DESIGN

  • Earthquake resistant framed structure with infill brick walls.
  • 1.5 mtr. wide stairs steps, 1.5 mtr. wide corridor.
  • Living room and bedroom with large covered balconies for sitouts.
02

STATUS

  • The entire project is approved by Himuda and freehold.
  • Non-Himachalis can own and invest in the project without any hassles.
  • Affordable friendly homes with global comforts.
03

CONVENIENCE

  • High-speed automatic lift in every block with automatic rescue device.
  • Kone, OTIS or equivalent make.
  • Convenience shops for daily needs in the vicinity.
04

LOCATION/CONNECTIVITY

  • Located in the Shivaliks on National Highway-5 enroute to Shimla.
  • Only an hour’s drive from Panchkula.
05

SCENIC VIEWS

  • Panoramic views of Kasauli & Dakshai Hills and Naahan Valley.
  • 6000 thousand feet height (Shimla 7000’).
  • Occasional snowfall at the site.
