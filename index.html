Welcome to The Woods Barog

Crafting Living Masterpieces

At The Woods Barog, we’re passionate about turning your Apartments into a true reflection of your personal style and a haven for relaxation and enjoyment.
Whether you’re dreaming of a vibrant floral display, a serene outdoor retreat, or a stunning landscape transformation.Nestled in the serene hills of Barog, The Woods Barog offers a perfect blend of luxury and tranquility. Our apartments provide breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, picturesque landscapes, and a calm ambiance—your ideal second home in Himachal Pradesh. Our Story

Crafting Beautiful Gardens Since '99

The Woods Barog is developed by R.V. Nirmata Private Limited, a company led by highly qualified professionals with decades of experience. Under the guidance of Sh. R.K. Bhalla, a renowned Architect from IIT Kharagpur with over 50 years of expertise, and Executive Director Sh. Jiten Bhalla, an Engineer from UDCT, Bombay, the company has delivered numerous quality projects. Their portfolio includes regional planning for townships like Greenfields, Faridabad, and DLF Phases I, II & III in Gurgaon, as well as designing and supervising over 80 school buildings, 50 industrial projects, and 200 homes in the NCR region. With a legacy of excellence, R.V. Nirmata Private Limited brings unparalleled expertise to creating luxurious and harmonious living spaces at The Woods Barog.

Our Commitment to Excellence

STRUCTURE & CUSTOMISATION OF DESIGN

  • Earthquake resistant framed structure with infill brick walls.
  • 1.5 mtr. wide stairs steps, 1.5 mtr. wide corridor.
  • Living room and bedroom with large covered balconies for sitouts.
02

STATUS

  • The entire project is approved by Himuda and freehold.
  • Non-Himachalis can own and invest in the project without any hassles.
  • Affordable friendly homes with global comforts.
03

CONVENIENCE

  • High-speed automatic lift in every block with automatic rescue device.
  • Kone, OTIS or equivalent make.
  • Convenience shops for daily needs in the vicinity.
04

LOCATION/CONNECTIVITY

  • Located in the Shivaliks on National Highway-5 enroute to Shimla.
  • Only an hour’s drive from Panchkula.

SCENIC VIEWS
